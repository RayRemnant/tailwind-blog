1. Press **Windows key + Shift + S**
2. The screen will darken and a bar will appear at the top
3. You can select 4 different modes: **Rectagular**, **Free-form**, **Window** or **Entire screen**
4. The default is rectangular mode, click and drag anywhere on the screen to draw your desired shape
5. **The result will be saved on the clipboard**
6. Open Paint and press **Ctrl + V** to paste the screenshot
7. Press **Ctrl + S** to save the image
